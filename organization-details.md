### **Overview of the Training Organization**  
Jaitechoon is a software training and consulting organization focused on building industry-ready engineering graduates. The organization delivers hands-on, project-oriented training that mirrors real-world software development environments. Students are guided through backend development, API design, cloud fundamentals, DevOps practices, and software engineering principles. The training emphasizes problem-solving, clean coding, and building complete end-to-end solutions using modern tools and technologies.
